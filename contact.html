Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about visiting our salon

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

While we welcome walk-ins, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure availability and avoid waiting times. You can book via WhatsApp, phone, or our online form.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

Please arrive 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. This allows for consultation and ensures you can relax and enjoy your experience without rushing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI, and mobile wallets. We also accept all major payment platforms for your convenience.

Is parking available at the salon?

Yes, we have dedicated parking space for our customers at no additional cost. Additional parking is also available in nearby areas.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 4 hours before the scheduled time without any charges. Please inform us via phone or WhatsApp.
