Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure availability and avoid waiting times.

What products do you use for treatments?

We use premium, internationally recognized brands like L'Oréal, Kerastase, and Dermalogica for all our treatments.

Do you offer bridal packages?

Yes, we offer comprehensive bridal packages including makeup, hair styling, and pre-bridal treatments. Contact us for customized packages.

What are your operating hours?

We're open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Do you have parking facilities?

Yes, we have dedicated parking space for our customers at no additional cost.
